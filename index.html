Focaccia with Caramelized Onins,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramilized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vages

featured in:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes that Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the toppping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it,s not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberies

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
  2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixed with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don,t need to actually knead the dougt, tought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incoporate the last of the flour...
